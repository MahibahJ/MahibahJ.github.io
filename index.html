About Me

I got into photography in middle school when I inherited my dad's old phone. I went around taking photos of everything and anything that interested me. When I entered high school I was given the chance to check out my own high quality DSLR camera from the photrapgy class I was enrolled in. Through that course I gained many new skills and was able to further develop my portfolio. After I graduated highschool my dad's bestfriend gifted me his old DSLR because he was more interested in go pros. When I entered university, I dind't have any time to go out and tak epictures, but I realized this is a hobby I want ot take more seriously and I was given the proper tools to continue. Now I bring my camera to campus on days when I don't have many classes to shoot some photos when I have time. I also expanded my interest into film cameras and in my free time try to fix cameras I've purchased from thrift stores. I'm not very good at it but I love tinkering with bits and the satisfaction I get from success outweighs any frustration I face along the way.
